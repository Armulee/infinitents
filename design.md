# Design System

## Philosophy

Beauty must never compromise usability.

The product should feel:

* Linear
* Stripe
* Arc Browser
* Apple

The experience should feel premium, intelligent, and fast.

---

## Design Principles

Users should feel:

* Control
* Momentum
* Confidence
* Progress

Never:

* AI gimmicky
* Cyberpunk
* Crypto
* Web3

---

## Responsive Design

Desktop First

Breakpoints:

* 320
* 375
* 768
* 1024
* 1440
* 1920

Desktop:

* Fixed Sidebar
* Multi-column

Tablet:

* Collapsible Sidebar

Mobile:

* Bottom Navigation
* Swipe Interactions

---

* Dashboard
* Content Queue
* Viral Lab

---

## Content Queue

Most important page.

Do not design like Airtable.

Do not design like Notion.

Design like:

TikTok × Linear

Desktop:

Centered feed.

One video at a time.

Mobile:

Full-screen swipe.

Actions:

* Approve
* Reject
* Request Changes
* Regenerate

Swipe Right:
Approve

Swipe Left:
Request Changes

Double Tap:
Quick Approve

---

## Motion

Use Framer Motion.

Animations should be:

* Fast
* Smooth
* Natural

Inspired by:

* Apple
* Linear
* Arc Browser

Motion should improve usability.

Not distract.
